Nami AI is an innovative project spearheaded by our team, focusing on training an advanced assistant powered by AI. This project aims to revolutionize how we interact with technology by enabling voice search capabilities and facilitating natural, human-like conversations with the AI. Through Nami AI, users can experience seamless, real-time interactions and receive answers as if they were conversing with a real person.
